Budget Tour Cairo, Aswan, Abu Simble, and Luxor Package From $980

Overview

Our Budget Egypt package is specially tailored for the fans of Pharaonic monuments, Ancient Egyptian History, and culture, the Cairo, Aswan, Abu Simble & Luxor Budget Package is one of the best Egypt packages that accommodate all travelers with a certain budget and would like to see as much as they can in our beautiful country Egypt. It offers our guests a wonderful chance to explore the most important monuments of Cairo like the Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx beside the Egyptian Museum of Antiquities. Then travel by the night sleeper train from Cairo to Aswan in order to experience the beauty of this amazing city and have the chance to see the wonderful sites there with a side trip to see the magnificent temple of Abu Simble, This is besides spending two nights to discover the secrets of the historical city of Luxor, and also explore the attractions situated there. This Custom tour to Egypt has the best balance between the scheduled tours included in the program and free time for the tourists to enjoy Egypt in their own style; with many optional tours offered all along the trip.

Itinerary

  • Day 1 Arrival at Cairo International Airport Arrive in Cairo – ETB representative your best friend in the coming few days will be waiting for you inside the arrival hall at Cairo airport holding a sign in hand with your names and will help you through immigration and customs then you will be transferred to start your tour. During your transfer, your tour manager will provide you with very helpful information that you will need during your trip to Egypt and you will have the chance to ask him any questions – Check in at your Hotel – Overnight in Cairo
  • Day 2 Pyramids Of Giza & Sphinx, Egyptian Museum Of Antiquities, Sleeper train to Aswan
  • Day 3 Arrive Aswan, Philae temple Of Isis, High Dam, Felucca ride around the Islands
  • Day 4 Overday trip to Abu Simble
  • Day 5 Drive to Luxor. Afternoon: East Bank tour “Karnak and Luxor”
  • Day 6 West Bank tour “Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut, Memnon”
  • Day 7 Fly back to Cairo
