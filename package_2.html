Budget Tour: Cairo, Aswan, Abu Simbel & Luxor

Experience Egypt’s iconic highlights from $980

Tour Overview

Designed for travelers who want maximum value without compromising on experience, this budget-friendly Egypt tour takes you through the country’s most iconic destinations: Cairo, Aswan, Abu Simbel, and Luxor.

Begin your journey in Cairo with visits to the Pyramids of Giza, the Sphinx, and the Egyptian Museum. Travel south via sleeper train to Aswan, where you’ll explore the High Dam, Philae Temple, and enjoy a traditional felucca ride. Continue to the magnificent Abu Simbel Temples, then head to Luxor to uncover the wonders of the East and West Banks, including the Valley of the Kings and Karnak Temple.

This itinerary offers the perfect balance between guided tours and free time, allowing you to explore Egypt at your own pace while benefiting from expert planning and local insights.

Itinerary Highlights

  • Day 1: Arrival in Cairo & hotel check-in
  • Day 2: Pyramids, Sphinx & Egyptian Museum – sleeper train to Aswan
  • Day 3: Aswan – Philae Temple, High Dam & Felucca ride
  • Day 4: Abu Simbel day trip
  • Day 5: Transfer to Luxor – Karnak & Luxor Temples
  • Day 6: Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut Temple & Colossi of Memnon
  • Day 7: Flight back to Cairo
