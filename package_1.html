8 Days Cairo,Luxor,and Aswan Tour Package From $1,230

Tour Details

Awesome tour to Cairo, Luxor, and Aswan that includes a private visit to the highlights of the three cities! We are very glad in fact to offer this tour to all our valued clients who wish to see the real face of ancient Egypt. We ask visitors who are planning a trip to Egypt to join us on this fantastic tour to the main cities of Egypt, Cairo, Luxor, and Aswan to see the impressive Pyramids, Temples, and royal temples as well. We also have a very interesting list with featured optional tours in the three cities that match all needs and requirements of our guests. The Land of the Pharaohs gives the traveler a chance to explore almost all the wonderful ancient Egyptian historical sites in the three cities that are richer in their monuments in Egypt; Cairo, the capital of Egypt, Luxor, which has around 1/3 of the world’s monuments and the amazing city of Aswan. This is besides having the chance to enjoy visiting the most popular highlights in the south of Egypt including Kom Ombo and Edfu. Our lovely tour in Egypt begins in Cairo, where visitors can visit the marvelous Pyramids of Giza, the ancient site of Saqqara, Memphis, The Egyptian Museum of Antiquities, the Citadel of Saladin, and Coptic Cairo “where visitors will see many of the historical churches of Egypt. Afterward, visitors who enjoy their vacation in Egypt will travel to Luxor by airplane to begin their tour in Upper Egypt. The tour in Luxor included visits to the Valley of the Kings, the Temple of Hatshepsut, the Colossi of Memnon, the Karnak Temples Complex, and Temple Temple of Luxor. After spending marvelous days in Luxor, we ride in our comfortable air-conditioned vehicle to reach Aswan. In our journey to Aswan, we will be exploring the historical temples of Kom Ombo and Edfu. In Aswan, we will be visiting The High Dam, the Unfinished Obelisk, and the amazing Temple of Philae. This tour package to Cairo, Luxor, and Aswan is certainly designed for those who are in love with ancient Egyptian history, art, and architecture. Featured with its value for money, the most wonderful accommodation in your chosen hotel, the Cairo, Luxor, and Aswan highlights package is among our most recommended tours.
