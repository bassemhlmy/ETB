8-Day Cairo, Luxor & Aswan Tour Package

Explore Egypt’s greatest ancient wonders from $1,230

Tour Overview

Discover the timeless beauty of Egypt on this expertly curated 8-day journey through Cairo, Luxor, and Aswan. This private tour takes you deep into the heart of ancient civilization, where history, culture, and architecture come to life.

Stand before the iconic Pyramids of Giza, explore the treasures of the Egyptian Museum, and wander through Old Cairo. Fly south to Luxor, home to nearly one-third of the world’s ancient monuments, where you’ll visit the Valley of the Kings, Karnak Temple, and more. Continue to Aswan, discovering the unique temples of Edfu and Kom Ombo along the way.

Designed for travelers who value comfort, authenticity, and expert guidance, this package combines top attractions, quality accommodations, and seamless transportation for a truly unforgettable Egyptian experience.

What's Included

  • 4 nights in Cairo (Bed & Breakfast)
  • 2 nights in Luxor (Bed & Breakfast)
  • 1 night in Aswan (Bed & Breakfast)
  • Domestic flights: Cairo → Luxor & Aswan → Cairo
  • Meet & assist at airports
  • Professional English-speaking Egyptologist guides
  • Entrance fees to all listed attractions
  • Private air-conditioned transportation
  • Lunch during Cairo tours

What's Not Included

  • International flights
  • Egypt entry visa
  • Personal expenses
  • Tipping (guide, driver, etc.)
  • Optional tours

Itinerary Highlights

  • Day 1: Arrival in Cairo & hotel check-in
  • Day 2: Pyramids of Giza, Saqqara & Memphis
  • Day 3: Egyptian Museum, Citadel & Old Cairo
  • Day 4: Fly to Luxor – Karnak & Luxor Temples
  • Day 5: Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut Temple & Colossi of Memnon
  • Day 6: Edfu & Kom Ombo Temples
  • Day 7: Aswan – High Dam, Philae Temple & Unfinished Obelisk
  • Day 8: Departure
