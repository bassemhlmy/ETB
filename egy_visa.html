Details about the Egypt Entry Visa

    To be able to enter Egypt and enjoy all the interesting sites and charming natural places there, you will need to get an Entry Visa. To enter Egypt with ease and comfort, we will tell you all the details that a traveler may need. The Visa is the formal document key that can allow non-citizens to enter a foreign country to spend a specific limited time. To enter Egypt, you can have one of these 3 different kinds of visas:

  • Free Visa
  • Visa upon arrival.
  • E-visa (Electronic visa).
  • Throughout this article, we will provide you with detailed information about each case for most of the countries to get their Visas to Egypt.

Countries can Apply for an E-Visa

Now, you can easily apply for an Egyptian visa online and get an E-Visa (electronic visa). You will be able to get the single entry visa or the multiple-entry visa, to choose what suits you here are the details for each visa: A Single entry Visa will allow you to stay for a maximum of 30 days, and it’s valid for 6 months from the day of issue. A multiple entry Visa will allow you to visit Egypt and leave then get back again as much as you want for almost 3 months, but you have to make sure that your stay in Egypt will not exceed the standard 30 days per visit. There are some requirements to get the Egyptian E-Visa online. Here is your guide:

  • Firstly, make sure that you have a valid passport with at least 8 months from the day you’re applying for the Visa.
  • Then you will find an online form that you need to fill out, but be careful to not make any errors.
  • Now, you will get to the paying step, where you can use your credit or debit card to pay the Visa fees.
  • Make sure to upload a scan photo of your passport's main page.
  • Finally, you have to get a copy of the e-Visa with you while reaching the Egyptian border upon your arrival.
