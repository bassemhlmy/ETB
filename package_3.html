Cairo & Sharm El Sheikh Holiday

Culture, history & Red Sea escape from $995

Tour Overview

Experience the perfect blend of Egypt’s ancient heritage and Red Sea relaxation on this unforgettable journey through Cairo and Sharm El Sheikh.

Begin in Cairo, where you’ll explore iconic landmarks including the Pyramids of Giza, the Sphinx, the Egyptian Museum, and the Citadel of Saladin. Then fly to Sharm El Sheikh, a world-class coastal destination known for its crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and laid-back luxury.

Enjoy four days of leisure in Sharm — relax on pristine beaches, explore the underwater world, or join optional desert and Bedouin experiences. This package is designed for travelers seeking both discovery and relaxation in one seamless trip.

What's Included

  • 2 nights in Cairo (Bed & Breakfast)
  • 4 nights in Sharm El Sheikh (Full Board)
  • Domestic flights: Cairo ⇄ Sharm El Sheikh
  • Meet & assist services
  • Professional Egyptologist guides in Cairo
  • Entrance fees to all listed attractions
  • Lunch during Cairo tours
  • Private air-conditioned transfers
  • Complimentary local SIM card

What's Not Included

  • International flights
  • Egypt entry visa
  • Personal expenses
  • Tipping (guide, driver, etc.)
  • Optional excursions

Itinerary Highlights

  • Day 1: Arrival in Cairo & hotel check-in
  • Day 2: Pyramids, Sphinx, Egyptian Museum & Citadel
  • Day 3: Flight to Sharm El Sheikh
  • Day 4–6: Free time in Sharm (beach, diving, optional tours)
  • Day 7: Return & departure
